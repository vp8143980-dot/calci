How calculations work

Exclusive price: GST = price × (rate/100). Total = price + GST.

Inclusive price: Base = price ÷ (1 + rate/100). GST = price − Base.

Intra-state: CGST = SGST = GST ÷ 2. Inter-state: IGST = GST.
